About Me

I am a PhD student majoring in theoretical physics in Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University. My interests include quantum information and quantum computation, cold atomic physics and condensed matter physics. My two major academic goals are realizing fault-tolerant quantum computation on cold atomic platforms, and understanding more about quantum information.

I love music. I like to listening to classical music, especially Beethoven and Brahms. I also play the piano and sing a little. Learning languages is also enjoyable to me. I am learning French and Japanese, and planning to learn Latin and Arabic later. Another hobby of mine is the so-called "minor subject", including historical phonology and philology of Chinese language. Most of the time, I relax myself by reading literature and history.

My name is pronounced as /wɑŋ35i51fei55/ in Mandarin. In Chinese, the family name comes first, so 'Wang' is my family name and 'Yifei' is my given name. In English, you can call me 'Fei'.
